The Monarch Dispatch is Now Online!
The Monarch Dispatch is the fictional newspaper of the Monarch Mystery Book Series, written by me, C.E. Lovick, and it's now online!
By: C.E. Lovick

The Monarch Dispatch is the fictional newspaper of the Monarch Mystery Book Series, written by me, C.E. Lovick, and it's now online! The website allows readers and fans to dive deeper into the world of the Monarch Mystery Series. Starting with book one, Trail of the Shapeshifter, our timeline begins on Saturday, September 14th, 1991. It continues into the future from that date. You can explore the city of Monarch, Wyoming, meet the characters from the books, and learn about other events beyond what happens in the novels.

Each day contains several different news stories. If you click on the "Go Forward in Time" button, you'll see how events progress that mirror those in the novels. You're encouraged to explore the website and click the "Submit News Story" to contact me about your questions or ideas. Thanks for coming here!

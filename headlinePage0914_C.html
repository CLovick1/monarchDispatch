35th Annual Monarch Awards Ceremony
The 35th Annual Awards Ceremony will take place at the Lotus Theatre, September 16th.

By: HENRY TILGRASS


The Lotus Theatre is preparing to host the 35th Annual Monarch Award Ceremony, set to take place on Monday, September 16th at 7:30pm. This highly anticipated event is a celebration of excellence in our community, recognizing the best businesses and individuals across a variety of categories.

This year's ceremony will showcase the best of the best, including the top restaurants, bars, and stores in town. Additionally, the event will recognize individual achievements in fields such as healthcare, construction, and trades, with awards for the best doctors, dentists, and tradespersons.

Nominees for the Monarch Awards were carefully selected based on their contributions to the community, exceptional customer service, and commitment to excellence. A panel of judges consisting of local business leaders and community members had the difficult task of choosing the winners from a pool of highly qualified candidates.

The Monarch Award Ceremony is an opportunity for the community to come together and celebrate the businesses and individuals who make our town a great place to live, work, and visit. Tickets are available now and can be purchased at the Lotus Theatre box office. Don't miss your chance to be a part of this unforgettable event.
